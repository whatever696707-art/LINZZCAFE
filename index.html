Our Story

A Place Built
on Good Days

LINZZ was born from a simple belief — that the best moments happen over a beautifully brewed cup of coffee and a table shared with the right people.

Nestled in the heart of Aurangabad, our café blends warm wooden tones with soft, organic textures — creating a space that feels both intimate and alive.

Bad day? Coffee. Good day? Coffee. Stressed? Coffee. Happy? Coffee. — that's our philosophy.
